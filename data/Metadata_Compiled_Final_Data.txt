Metadata for Compiled_Final_Data.csv

Compiled_Final_Data.csv contains all of the data related to denitrification rates across vegetation zones and month during 2018 at the Barn Island Wildlife Management Area (BIWMA) in Stonington, CT.

Contact: Kayleigh Granville (keg8fb@virginia.edu) and Ashley Helton (ashley.helton@uconn.edu)


List of abbreviations and units organized by column: 

SampleID: unique sample id given by salt marsh zone (LM, HM, PH) and replicate number (1-5).  

Date: Sampling month  

Tin: tin tare weight in units are grams (g)

WetSoil: wet soil weight in units are grams (g)

TinPlusWetSoil: units are grams (g)

TinPlusDrySoil: units are grams (g)

TinPlusAshedSoil: units are grams (g)

Water: units are grams (g)

DrySoil: units are grams (g)

AshedSoil: units are grams (g)

LOI: units are (g ashed / g dry)

DWE:  units are (g dry / g wet)

SoilMoistureFraction:

NH4_ppmN: units (ppm N)

NH4_Detect: NH4 ppm N with 1/2MDL, where units are ppm N

ExtractedWetWeight: KCl extract soil weight, wet (g)

ExtractedDryWeight: KCl extract soil weight, dry (g)

NH4_mgN: units are mg N

NH4_normalized: NH4 mg N per g dry soil

WaterExtractedWetWeight: Water extract soil weight, wet (g)

WaterExtractedDryWeight: Water extract soil weight, dry (g)

SO4_ppm: units are X

SO4_mg: units are X

SO4_normalized: SO4 mg per g dry soil

DenitRate: Denit (ng N / hr / g dry soil)

N2ORate: N2O (ng / hr / g dry soil)

N2OYield: unitless
